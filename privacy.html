Privacy Policy

At Lucu LLC, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and sharing of your personal information. Please read this document carefully to understand our stance on your privacy.

1. No Collection of Personal Information

Lucu LLC does not collect any personal information from users. We do not track, store, or process any personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or IP addresses. Your interactions with our services remain completely anonymous.

2. No Use of Cookies or Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any other tracking technologies to monitor your activity on our website or services. Your browsing experience is not monitored or recorded in any way.

3. No Sharing of User Data

Since we do not collect any user information, there is no user data to share with third parties. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any personal information to external parties, including advertisers, partners, or other entities.

4. Data Security

Although we do not collect user information, we are committed to ensuring that any data associated with the operation of our services, such as server logs and technical data, are handled with the utmost security and confidentiality. Our servers and infrastructure are secured with industry-standard measures to prevent unauthorized access.

5. Changes to This Policy

Lucu LLC reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised policy on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our privacy practices.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please feel free to contact us at:
